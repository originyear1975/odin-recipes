One-Pot Crispy Chicken and Rice

This meal is so comforting and family friendly

One-Pot Crispy Chicken and Rice is a savory and comforting dish that is easy to make in just one pot. The recipe typically involves browning bone-in chicken pieces in oil until crispy and flavorful, then removing them from the pot and sautéing onions and garlic until fragrant. Rice is added and toasted in the same pot before liquid is added to cook the rice and chicken until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through.

Vegetables such as peas or carrots can be added for additional flavor and nutrition. The end result is a one-pot meal that is hearty and full of flavor. The crispy chicken adds a delicious crunch, while the rice is cooked in a flavorful liquid that is absorbed during the cooking process. One-Pot Crispy Chicken and Rice is a great option for a comforting meal for any occasion, and it's perfect for busy weeknights when you want something delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 6 (4 ounce) bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • ¾ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • ⅔ cup finely chopped yellow onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 ½ cups uncooked long-grain rice
  • 3 cups chicken stock, divided, or more to taste
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 1 medium lemon, cut into wedges

Cooking Directions

  1. Step One

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  2. Step Two

    Heat oil in large Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Sprinkle chicken thighs evenly with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.

  3. Step Four

    Toss tater tots and seasoned salt in a bowl to combine. Arrange tater tots decoratively over beef mixture in the skillet, avoiding large gaps between tots.

  4. Step Five

    Bake in the preheated oven until tater tots are golden and crisp, about 1 hour. Sprinkle with cheese and scallions; cool 10 minutes before serving.
